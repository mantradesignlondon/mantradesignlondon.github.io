Seema Barot Founder of MDL is a UK-renowned British interior designer specializing in bespoke high-end interior design. She has over 30 years of experience in the industry, who with her simple yet opulent style, has permeated interior design at every level. Seema, as well as designing luxury apartments & homes for an ever-expanding international private client list, designs and manufactures products for the interior design industry globally. She also designs ranges of home accessories, furniture, carpets, fabrics and bed linens, which are totally bespoke and can be produced also in small quantities for clients both in the residential and hospitality industry.

INDIA'S LEADING PROVIDER OF ASSAM SILK.
Eris have taken the beautiful, traditional hand-spun Assam silk and sculpted it to Eri Silk; A finer, more durable yarn, that is perfect for both the fashion and home furnishing markets.

ERIS SILK IS 100% CRUELTY FREE.
All the fabrics are manufactured in India and are 100% Silk or a blend of cotton, linen and silk. Eris is 100% cruelty free as the manufacturing process does not involve the killing of silkworms. The fibre is extracted from open-mouthed cocoons where silkworms are allowed to grow into moths. The aligns with the global trend of 'Slow Fashion', a movement towards sustainable, natural materials instead of artificially cultivated ones.

We can customize products and have systems in place to make high- quality yarns and fabrics, quickly and efficiently.
